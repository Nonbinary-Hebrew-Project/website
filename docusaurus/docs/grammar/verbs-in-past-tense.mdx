## Standard verbs
## <HB> שלמים </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> לָמַדְתֶה </HB>|you learned|
|2nd person plural|<HB> לְמַדְתֶמֵן </HB>|you all learned|
|3rd person singular|<HB> לָמְדֶה </HB>|they learned|

## Verbs whose root ends in heh
## <HB> ל’’ה </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> עָשִׂיתֶה </HB>|you made|
|2nd person plural|<HB> עֲשִׂיתֶמֵן </HB>|you all made|
|3rd person singular|<HB> עָשְׂתֶה </HB>|they made|

## Verbs whose root has a yud or vav as the second letter
## <HB> ע’’ו/ע’’י </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> קַמְתֶה </HB>|you rose|
|2nd person plural|<HB> קַמְתֶמֵן </HB>|you all rose|
|3rd person singular|<HB> קָמֶה </HB>|they rose|
