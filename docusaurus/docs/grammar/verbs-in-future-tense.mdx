## Standard verbs
## <HB> שלמים </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> תִּלְמְדֶה </HB>|you will learn|
|3rd person singular|<HB> יִלְמְדֶה </HB>|they will learn|

## Verbs whose root ends in heh
## <HB> ל’’ה </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> תַּעֲשֶׂאָה </HB>|you will make|
|3rd person singular|<HB> יַעֲשֶׂאָה </HB>|they will make|

## Verbs whose root has a yud or vav as the second letter
## <HB> ע’’ו/ע’’י </HB>
|Form|Hebrew Example|Translation|
|----|-------------|------------|
|2nd person singular|<HB> תָּקוּמֶה </HB>|you will rise|
|3rd person singular|<HB> יָקוּמֶה </HB>|they will rise|
