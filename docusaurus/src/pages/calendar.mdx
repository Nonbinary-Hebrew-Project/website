# Upcoming Events

## Collaborative Language Laboratory Workshop

Come join the Nonbinary Hebrew Project and folks who use it in a community-based "hackathon"! In this collaborative language laboratory, we welcome you to a) bring something you are working on putting into nonbinary Hebrew using our system, or b) bring your enthusiasm and community mindset to work with others on their projects. We will be on Zoom and will split into breakout rooms for the various projects folks bring or opt into, with Eyal and Lior available as resource for questions, as well as your fellow lab-mates. This event will be 1.5 hrs long, on April 30th from 5:30 PM EST - 7 PM EST.

[Register to join us on Zoom](https://cuboulder.zoom.us/meeting/register/8ju8rjEHQjGcLhToWty9pA)

## Introductory Workshop
Come learn about the Nonbinary Hebrew Project! In this workshop we will cover what the project is, how it started, and how to use some of the grammar system. This workshop is open to people of all Hebrew levels, and there will be two breakout rooms: one to go over the grammar with Eyal and one to discuss gender, grammar, and Judaism in English with Lior.
This event will be 1.5 hrs long, on May 17th from 4 pm EST - 5:30 PM EST
